Myth vs. Fact

MYTH: Linking my Aadhaar is enough to get my scholarship.

FACT: Linking is for ID only. You must ALSO get your account "Seeded for DBT" by the bank to receive funds.

MYTH: If I have multiple accounts linked, the government will choose one.

FACT: The money goes to the LATEST account you have seeded for DBT. Ensure your primary account is the last one you seeded.
